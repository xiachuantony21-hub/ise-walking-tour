Why walk with us

Four reasons we are not like the others.

USP 01 · 正統性
Traditional Pilgrimage Order
外宮 → 内宮 — the way it has been done for 1,500 years

Pilgrims have visited Gekū (Outer Shrine) before Naikū (Inner Shrine) for over a millennium. We walk Ise the way the ancients walked it — and explain why the order matters.

USP 02 · 学び
Deep Cultural Learning at Our Shop
日本 · 神道 · 伊勢 · 三重 · 日本人の習慣

A 15-minute briefing inside our shop covers Shintō, Ise, Mie, and everyday Japanese customs. By the time you reach the torii, you understand what you are about to see.

USP 03 · 手ぶら
Hands-Free Pilgrimage
手ぶら参拝 — 3 minutes from Iseshi Station

Drop your suitcases at our shop, slip into a samue, walk Ise unburdened. No coin lockers, no detours. Collect everything at 16:45.

USP 04 · 食
Walk & Eat Local Traditional Snacks
食べ歩き — a culture rarely allowed elsewhere

Walk-and-eat is discouraged in most of Japan — but Okage Yokochō was built for it. Matsusaka beef skewers, Ise udon, tekone-zushi, akafuku mochi, fresh oysters. A snack crawl, not a sit-down lunch.

The Sacred Route

A 4-hour walking pilgrimage.

12:00 – 12:30
Meet & greet at our shop. Drop your luggage, relax, and explore the books and videos on Mie's hidden gems — onsens, mountains, fishing villages, festivals, and where locals actually go.
12:30 – 12:45
Deep cultural briefing — 15 minutes inside the shop on Japan, Shintō, Ise, Mie, and Japanese customs. Then we step outside.
12:45 – 13:00
Walk down Geku Sandō, the historic pilgrim approach.
13:00 – 13:30
Gekū (外宮) — the Outer Shrine. Visited first, per 1,500 years of pilgrimage tradition. Toyouke Ōmikami, deity of food, clothing & shelter.
13:35 – 13:55
Mie Kōtsū bus: 外宮前 → 内宮前 (¥520, ~12 min, paid in cash on board).
14:00 – 14:40
Naikū (内宮) — the Inner Shrine. Cross Uji Bridge, walk along the Isuzu River, stand before Amaterasu Ōmikami — the most sacred site in Japan.
14:40 – 15:45
Okage Yokochō — Walk & Eat traditional snacks. Matsusaka beef skewers, Ise udon, tekone-zushi, akafuku mochi, fresh oysters. Pay-as-you-go.
15:50 – 16:15
Bus back from 内宮前 to Iseshi area. Final reflections en route.
16:15 – 16:45
Return to Real Japan by YamaTrips. Take your time browsing omamori, samue, stickers & Mie crafts. Collect your luggage. Tour closes at 16:45.
